Our Team

At Mix & Gro, our team’s expertise expands far beyond the talented colleagues in our office. Our business development model is centered on assembling a diverse group professionals and companies who all add value to our customers.

Our substrate suppliers follow rigorous standard operation procedures to ensure us that their plant grow media meets the high-quality product standards which we offer to you.

We only want to sell you what you need and not what we have. Our network of advisors helps us develop products to fit your needs. Mix & Gro works with an integral group of soil blenders that can blend our substrates with any combination of plant grow substances for your unique requirements.

Our broad relationships with trucking companies allows us flexibility to get our products to you when it’s convenient for you.
