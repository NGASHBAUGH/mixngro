What We Offer

100% Spent Mushroom
Substrate (SMS)

Mix & Gro, LLC entered into an exclusive Master Distributor Agreement with a CCOF and CDFA Certified Mushroom Farm in California. Our agreement allows us to private label the spent mushroom substrate that the farm generates after they clean out their mushroom growing rooms.

SMS is derived from a unique compost blend specifically formulated to grow white button and crimini mushrooms — and it's one of the most nutrient-rich soil amendments available to California growers.

CCOF & CDFA Certified

Sourced from a California-certified organic mushroom farm, fully compliant for organic agriculture operations.

Private Label Available

Exclusive master distributor agreement means we can private label product for your brand or operation.

Custom Blending
